  <section class="brand-section">
    <h2>Core rules</h2>
    <div class="brand-grid">
      <div class="brand-panel">
        <h3>The domain is the product signal.</h3>
        <p>Short-link systems succeed when the domain is recognizable, readable, and trusted. Let the domain wordmark lead generated public pages.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="brand-panel">
        <h3>System identity stays quiet.</h3>
        <p>vanityURLs branding should confirm the redirect system and support the operator brand, not compete with the destination.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="brand-panel">
        <h3>Use transparent SVG badges.</h3>
        <p>Badges stay flexible across light, dark, and operator-owned surfaces when they remain vector, localized, and background-free.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="brand-panel">
        <h3>Keep copy operational.</h3>
        <p>Write plainly. Prefer concrete status, security, privacy, and lifecycle language over marketing claims.</p>
      </div>
    </div>

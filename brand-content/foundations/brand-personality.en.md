The vanityURLs personality is calm, precise, useful, transparent, and quietly technical.

## Calm

The system should reduce anxiety around redirects. Use clear states, predictable layouts, and measured language. Avoid dramatic warnings unless there is a real security or availability concern.

## Precise

Short links are small, but mistakes can be expensive. Use exact labels for domains, aliases, targets, expiration rules, languages, and blocking behavior.

## Useful

Every visual choice should help someone scan, compare, configure, or verify. Decoration is acceptable only when it supports recognition or comprehension.

## Transparent

Explain how generated pages, badges, analytics, and redirects behave. Do not hide important mechanics behind friendly copy.

## Quietly technical

The brand can show code, diagrams, hashes, paths, and configuration because the audience includes operators and contributors. Keep those details organized and readable instead of performative.

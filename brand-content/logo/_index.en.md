Use this section to choose the right vanityURLs logo asset and understand what is still missing from the brand kit.

Keep logo pages focused on usage standards, preview context, and required export sizes. Put packaged downloads, ZIP bundles, and broad asset inventories under [Resources](/resources/downloads/).

## Current logo groups

| Group             | Use                                                            |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Product logo      | Main vanityURLs project and product identity                   |
| Instance logo     | Operator-owned short-link instances and generated public pages |
| Redirected badges | Localized "Redirected by vanityURLs.link" attribution          |
| Website logos     | Header logos, favicon, app icons, and social previews          |

## Missing inputs

To complete the logo kit, provide source artwork in SVG where possible and PNG exports for raster-only targets. See the child pages for the specific sizes and file types.

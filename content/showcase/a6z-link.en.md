Prepared before the public launch, this instance gives an early-stage startup a stable short-link layer for internal references, partner handoffs, and future brand moments without exposing the product before it is ready.

Its redirect map can evolve privately in source control while the team shapes the public surface, keeping launch-day links durable instead of improvised at the last minute.

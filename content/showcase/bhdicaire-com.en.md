bhdicaire.com is the personal vanity domain of [Benoît H. Dicaire](https://github.com/bhdicaire), founder and primary maintainer of vanityURLs. It was the first deployment that proved the concept worked, predating the project's open-source release.

All links are managed via the `lnk` CLI script and deployed automatically through Cloudflare Pages on every push to the main branch.
